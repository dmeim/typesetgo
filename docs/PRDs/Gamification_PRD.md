# Feature: Gamification & Social

## 1. Overview
Engagement features to motivate students through friendly competition and recognition.

## 2. Leaderboards
* **Scopes**:
    * **Class**: Ranked list of students within a class.
    * **Global**: Anonymized top scores across the platform (opt-in).
* **Metrics**: Speed (WPM), Accuracy, Streaks.
* **Timeframes**: Daily, Weekly, All-time.
* **Privacy**:
    * Opt-out capability.
    * Global boards use pseudonyms or initials by default.

## 3. Events & Competitions
* **Events**: Teachers can schedule specific "Race" windows.
* **Live Dashboard**: Real-time leaderboard updates during an event (e.g., projected in class).
* **Recaps**: Auto-generated summary cards ("Top Speed", "Most Improved") to share.

## 4. Achievements (Future)
* Badges for milestones (e.g., "1000 Words Typed", "100% Accuracy Streak").
